# Chayan Project Walkthrough - Loom Script

Hey everyone, I’m thrilled to introduce you to Chayan—an AI-driven interview platform designed to make candidate selection smarter, faster, and more objective. Chayan, which literally means "Selection," is built to solve the massive bottleneck in early-stage hiring by automating the screening of hundreds of candidates while maintaining a deep focus on soft skills.

In this walkthrough, I’ll take you through the entire ecosystem, covering the three main roles: the Candidate, the Recruiter, and the Admin.

Let’s start with the Candidate experience. It all begins when a candidate receives a unique invite link, which uses a secure token-based system to land them on our interview portal at /interview/[token]. After a quick identity check, they move into the consent flow at /api/interviews/[sessionId]/consent. Once they’ve accepted the terms, the interview officially begins. The platform presents role-specific questions sequentially, and the candidate records their answers directly in the browser. We’ve built a custom useMediaRecorder hook to handle the audio capture and a heartbeat-based session tracker at /api/interviews/[sessionId]/heartbeat to ensure that even if the connection drops, the session remains secure and progress is saved. As soon as a candidate finishes an answer, it’s uploaded to our backend via /api/answers/upload, triggering a background processing chain while the candidate moves seamlessly to the next question.

Now, let’s switch gears to the Recruiter’s perspective. After signing in at /auth/signin, recruiters land on a high-level dashboard at /recruiter. Here, they can see a real-time list of all active and completed interviews. For each session, Chayan provides a comprehensive deep dive at /recruiter/interviews/[sessionId]. Instead of listening to hours of audio, recruiters get an automated Final Report. This report includes a recommendation—Move Forward, Hold, or Decline—backed by a multi-dimensional score across six critical soft-skill traits: Communication Clarity, Concept Explanation, Empathy, Adaptability, Professionalism, and English Fluency. Recruiters can play back the signed audio clips, read the transcripts generated by Sarvam or OpenAI, and then finalize their decision, which is saved via our /api/recruiter/interviews/[sessionId] POST endpoint.

Finally, we have the Admin layer, which is where the real power of Chayan is configured. Admins can manage the entire question bank at /admin/questions and monitor system health at /admin/metrics. But the most unique feature is our Calibration tool at /admin/calibration. This allows Admins to tune the AI’s evaluation logic by providing "Gold Standard" samples. They can input transcripts and manual scores, which the system then uses to calibrate the AI’s grading rubrics, ensuring that the automated evaluations stay perfectly aligned with the company’s specific hiring standards.

Under the hood, Chayan is powered by a robust and modern tech stack. We’re using Next.js with the App Router and TypeScript for a snappy, type-safe experience across all routes. Our data layer sits on Prisma and PostgreSQL, and we’ve integrated multiple AI providers, including Google, OpenAI, and Sarvam, to handle both general and localized transcription and evaluation. The real secret sauce, though, is our event-driven architecture using Inngest. Every time an answer is uploaded, Inngest orchestrates a complex background workflow: first transcribing the audio, then running it through our detailed evaluation rubrics, and finally synthesizing a summary report. This means the UI stays lightning-fast because the heavy lifting happens asynchronously.

One of the biggest strengths of Chayan is its ability to remain objective and scalable. By scoring candidates on specific rubrics like "Empathy and Patience" or "Adaptability," we remove much of the unconscious bias that often creeps into manual screening. And because of our decoupled background processing, we can handle a surge of thousands of applicants simultaneously without breaking a sweat.

Of course, building this wasn't without its hurdles. One major challenge was ensuring audio reliability across different browsers and network conditions. We solved this by implementing a robust retry mechanism for uploads and using signed URLs for secure, performant media delivery. We also spent a lot of time on AI consistency, which led to the creation of the Calibration system I mentioned earlier—allowing us to solve the "black box" problem of AI grading by making it tunable and transparent.

Looking ahead, we’re excited to take Chayan even further. Our next steps include adding live, interactive AI follow-up questions that adapt in real-time based on a candidate's previous answer, and building deeper integrations with popular Applicant Tracking Systems like Greenhouse and Lever. 

Chayan is more than just a screening tool; it’s about finding the right talent with precision, speed, and empathy. Thanks for watching!
